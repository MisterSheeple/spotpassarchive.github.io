SpotPass Archival Project / 3DS Guide (CFW)

Prerequisites:

  • Any 2DS/3DS running Luma3DS CFW
    • To check whether or not you have a CFW, while your system is off, hold down SELECT and then press the POWER button. If some text immediately appears on the top screen, then you have a CFW. Otherwise, that means you don't have a CFW and you need to go back and select a non-CFW option.
  • An SD card
  • A device that has an SD/microSD card reader
  1. Download the latest release of SpotPassDumper9 for Luma, available from Universal-Updater or as a release on GitHub.
    • When downloading from GitHub, extract the contents of the .zip file onto your SD/microSD card.
  2. While your system is powered off, hold START + POWER to boot into the Luma3DS chainloader.
  3. Select SpotPassDumper9 and press A.
  4. Follow the directions shown on the bottom screen.
  5. When done, the file partitionA.bin (or in rare cases, also partitionB.bin) will be located on your SD/microSD card in the spotpass_cache folder.
  6. Submit your dump using the green link below.
