SpotPass Archival Project / 3DS Guide (CFW)

Prerequisites:

  • Any 2DS/3DS running Luma3DS CFW
    • To check whether or not you have a CFW, while your system is off, hold down SELECT and then press the POWER button. If some text immediately appears on the top screen, then you have a CFW. Otherwise, that means you don't have a CFW and you need to go back and select a non-CFW option.
  • An SD card
  • A device that has an SD/microSD card reader
  1. Download the latest release of SpotPassDumper11, available from Universal-Updater or as a release on GitHub.
    • When downloading from GitHub, extract the contents of the .zip file onto your SD/microSD card.
  2. Install it to your HOME Menu using your preferred title manager or, if you wish to, you can also simply open it from the Homebrew Launcher.
    • When downloading via Universal-Updater, it will always be installed to your HOME Menu.
  3. Select SpotPassDumper11 and press A.
  4. Follow the directions shown on the (todo: which?) screen.
  5. If your system is connected to the internet, SpotPassDumper11 will attempt to upload your SpotPass cache directly to us. If it is unable to upload your cache to the internet, the file partitionA.bin (or in rare cases, also partitionB.bin) will be located on your SD/microSD card in the spotpass_cache folder.
