SpotPass Archival Project / Wii U Guide

Prerequisites:

  • Any modded/unmodded Wii U system with a system firmware version from 5.5.0 to 5.5.6 (latest)
  • An SD card formatted to FAT32
  1. Open the Internet Browser and navigate to https://dumplingapp.com
  2. Click on the "Launch Dumpling" button.
  3. Once Dumpling is open, use the D-Pad to select the option that says "Dump all SpotPass data" and press A.
  4. Select your SD card with the D-Pad, then select "OK" and press A.
  5. If your system is connected to the internet, Dumpling will attempt to upload your SpotPass cache directly to us. If it is unable to upload your cache to the internet, the task.db files will be located on your SD/microSD card in the Dumpling/SpotPass Files/ folder.
  6. If your system was unable to be upload your dump, you can also submit it using the green link below.
