SpotPass Archival Project / Wii U Guide (CFW)

Prerequisites:

  • Any Wii U system
  • An SD card formatted to FAT32
  1. Open the Internet Browser and navigate to https://dumplingapp.com
  2. Click on the "Launch Dumpling" button.
  3. Once Dumpling is open, use the D-Pad to select the option that says "SpotPass data" [TODO] and press A.
  4. Select your SD card with the D-Pad, then select "OK" and press A.
  5. When done, the files will be automatically uploaded to the server. If the files were unable to be uploaded automatically, they will be put on your SD card.
  6. If the upload failed, submit your dump using the green link below.
